Cookies Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

What cookies does this website use?

Necessary technical cookies
Essential for the website to function, such as remembering the language you have selected. They do not require your consent.
Analytical cookies (Google Analytics)
We use Google Analytics to track the number of visits anonymously. These cookies are only activated if you accept their use.

How to manage cookies?

You can accept or reject analytical cookies in the banner that appears when you enter the website. You can also configure your browser to block all cookies, although this may affect the website's functionality.

More information
